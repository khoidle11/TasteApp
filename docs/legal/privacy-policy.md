## Purpose

The Privacy Policy should explain what personal data TasteApp collects, how it is used, where it is shared, how long it is retained, and what choices users have.

## Data Categories

TasteApp may collect:

- Account data from Clerk and local TasteApp User profiles.
- Dish Reviews, Review Photos, Reports, Saved Items, and Catalog Contributions.
- Location inputs used for distance filters, Geocoded Locations, and list-first results.
- Device, log, analytics, and security data.
- Moderation history and review-integrity signals.
- Payment and entitlement data if premium features are added later.

## Product Uses

TasteApp may use data to:

- Authenticate users and protect account-required flows.
- Display Dish Reviews, RestaurantDish rankings, Saved Items, and public SEO pages.
- Store and moderate user-submitted Restaurants, Locations, Canonical Dishes, Menu Items, RestaurantDishes, and Review Photos.
- Prevent spam, fake reviews, coordinated review attacks, copyright infringement, and abuse.
- Improve search, rankings, recommendations, product quality, and support.

## Location Notes

MVP location UX is list-first. The policy should distinguish user-provided search location, stored Restaurant Locations, and any future precise device location permission.

## Review Photos

Review Photos are Phase 2. The final policy should explain photo metadata, object storage, moderation before display, deletion behavior, and copyright/takedown handling.

## User Rights And Choices

The final policy should cover account deletion, content deletion, data export, location permission controls, marketing choices, and jurisdiction-specific rights where applicable.

## Legal Review Notes

Counsel should determine whether TasteApp needs GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, children's privacy, biometric, geolocation, or international transfer language before launch.
